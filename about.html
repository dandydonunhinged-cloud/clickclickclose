The Mortgage Industry Has a Matching Problem

Every day, real estate investors lose deals — not because the deal is bad, but because they're talking to the wrong lender. Click Click Close exists to end that.

The Problem Nobody Talks About

Here's what happens to most real estate investors: You find a deal. You call a lender. They say yes. You send documents. Two weeks later, they find a reason to say no. You start over with another lender. Another two weeks. Another no.

The deal dies — not because it was bad, but because you were matched with the wrong lender. The wrong program. The wrong overlay. The wrong underwriting calculation.

Most brokers know 3-5 lenders. They send your deal to whoever answers the phone first. If that lender can't do it, you're starting from scratch. Meanwhile, your seller is getting other offers and your earnest money clock is ticking.

This is the matching problem. And it costs investors billions in lost deals every year.
