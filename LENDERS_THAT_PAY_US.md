## HOW WE GET PAID

Two models. Both legal. Choose per deal:

**Model A — Lender-Paid (YSP):** Lender pays us 0.5-2% of loan amount. Built into the rate. Borrower sees no separate broker fee. We earn by delivering the deal at a rate above wholesale par.

**Model B — Borrower-Paid:** We charge the borrower directly (0.5-2.75% of loan amount). Borrower gets a lower rate because no YSP is built in. More transparent.

**Cannot do both on the same loan** (Dodd-Frank / CFPB Loan Originator Compensation Rule).

**CRE Commercial loans (5+ units, non-residential):** Less regulated than residential. Broker fee is typically 0.5-1% lender-paid referral, or 1-2% borrower-paid. On a $5M deal at 1% = $50,000.
