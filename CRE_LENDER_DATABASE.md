All lenders below have been verified as either nationwide lenders or having specific Texas operations. Don operates from Texas (77651 zip / Beaumont-Port Arthur area).

**Licensing note:** Texas requires licensing for business-purpose residential mortgage loans (non-owner occupied). Commercial real estate loans on 5+ unit properties and non-residential commercial properties are generally exempt from RMLO licensing when structured as business-purpose loans. Verify current TREC and SML requirements before originating.
